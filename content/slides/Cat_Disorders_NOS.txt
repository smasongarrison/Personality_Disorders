\frame{
\frametitle{Not Otherwise Specified}
\begin{itemize}\item{Other specified personality disorder and unspecified personality disorder} ``is a category provided for two situations: \begin{enumerate}\item the individual's personality pattern meets the general criteria for a personality disorder, and traits of several different personality disorders are present, but the criteria for any specific personality disorder are not met;\\~\\\item  the individual's personality pattern meets the general criteria for a personality disorder, but the individual is considered to have a personality disorder that is not included in the DSM$-$5 classification (e.g., passive-aggressive personality disorder).''\end{enumerate}
\end{itemize}
